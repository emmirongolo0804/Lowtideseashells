Website Goals

The purpose of this website is to showcase and document my personal collection of seashells from around the world. This project is the result of several years of passionate exploration and research. Each specimen was either collected during my own trips to the beach or bought from random people and thrift stores, offering a unique glimpse into global shell circulation. Moreover, all shells were found naturally empty, and no animals were harmed during the collection process.

Collection Standards

In my seashell collection, I only store specimens that are either in perfect or good condition, fossilized, or naturally worn in color but structurally intact. I do not include shells that are broken, chipped, porous, covered in barnacles, or too difficult to clean properly. This standard helps maintain a consistent level of aesthetic and scientific value across the collection, focusing on quality and preservation.
