WEBSITE GOALS

The purpose of this website is to showcase and document my personal collection of seashells from around the world. This project is the result of several years of passionate exploration and research. Each specimen was either collected during my own trips to the beach or found at thrift stores, offering a unique glimpse into global shell circulation. Moreover, all shells were found naturally empty, and no animals were harmed during the collection process.
